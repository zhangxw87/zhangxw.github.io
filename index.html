Xiaowei Zhang

Senior Algorithm Development Engineer

OmniVision Technologies, Inc., Singapore

About me: I am a senior algorithm development engineer at OmniVision Technologies (OVT), Singapore. Prior to joining OVT on March of 2017, I have been working with Li Cheng as a senior post doctoral research fellow at the Bioinformatics Institute (BII) of A*STAR. I completed my PhD in computational mathematics at National University of Singapore under the supervision of Delin Chu in 2013. Before that, I graduated from East China Normal University in Shanghai, China, with a Bachelor's degree in computational mathematics in 2008.

Research interests: My current research interests include fast dimensionality reduction, machine learning and its applications to computer vision, matrix computations with applications to large-scale data analysis, numerical optimizations, and randomized numerical linear algebra.

See also my curriculum vitae, and Google Scholar.
